Welcome to Branch's **Marketer Hub**, your place to find resources for marketers leveraging Branch's Growth Platform to drive engagement, optimize campaign performance, and activate customers across channels.

## What is Marketer Hub?

Marketer Hub is a dedicated documentation workspace designed for marketers, growth teams, and campaign managers using Branch. Whether you're creating personalized link campaigns, measuring ad performance, or driving app adoption, you'll find guides, best practices, and insights here to help drive your success.

This hub focuses on the marketer experience, from campaign setup and optimization to analytics and reporting, without requiring technical implementation or coding knowledge.

## How to navigate

Use the left sidebar to browse topics, or use the search function (CMD+K) to quickly find specific information. Each section contains detailed guides, step-by-step instructions, and best practices to help you get the most from Branch's platform.

Article folders are organized primarily by feature, making it easy to compare what you see in Branch with the structure of Marketer Hub.
