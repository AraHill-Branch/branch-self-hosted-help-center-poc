## Overview

Facebook Business Manager allows you to manage several Facebook Ad Accounts within a single dashboard.

::: warning Required Permissions
You will need to be an Administrator on your Facebook Business Manager account.
:::

## 1. Enable Branch on your Facebook Dashboard

1. On your Facebook dashboard, select **Events Manager** > **Partner Integrations**.



2. Select your Branch App.



3. Click **Continue** and follow the on-screen prompts to connect your Facebook Ad-account with Branch.

## 2. Retrieve your Facebook Developers App ID

1. Go to the [Developers Facebook page](https://developers.facebook.com/).
2. Select your App from the dropdown menu.



3. Navigate to **Setting** > **Basic** and select the **App ID** & **Secret Key** copy and paste these to the relevant section on the Branch dashboard.



## 3. Authorize Your Facebook Ad Account and Business Manager

1. Navigate to **Advanced Settings**.
2. Scroll down to the **Access** section.
3. Under the **Business Manager** and **Facebook Ad-Accounts** section enter the **BM ID** and the **Facebook Ad-Account ID**.



## 4. Check Settings in Facebook Ads Manager

1. Navigate to **Ad Account Settings**.
2. Either via the access manager on your Facebook Ads Manager Dashboard or by clicking the gear icon on the top right corner of the screen.



I3. In the **Ad Account Setup** window you will observe the Timezone and the attribution window of your ad account. If you are not able to see these options please contact Facebook Support



## 5. Enable Facebook in Your Branch Dashboard

You can now enable Facebook as an Ad Partner in your Branch dashboard!

Please follow our guide for [Facebook App Install Ads](facebook-app-install-ads.md).
