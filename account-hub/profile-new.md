## Overview

Use the **Profile** tab on the **Manage account** page to manage your Branch account. This includes your name, role, preferences, and more. You can also view your user ID and account email.

## Open the Profile tab

Follow these steps to open the **Profile** tab:

1. In Branch, select the app/profile drop-down menu in the top-left corner of the page.
2. Select **Manage account** below your name in the drop-down menu.

## Update profile

On the **Profile** tab in the **Manage account** section in Branch, you can update your name, avatar, and user ID.

### Update your name

Your name is the name that will appear in Branch and the name that automated emails from Branch will be addressed to.

Follow these steps to update your Branch account name:

1. On the **Profile** tab, edit your name in the **Your name** text box.
2. Select **Save changes**.

### Update your avatar

Your avatar is how you'll appear in Branch.

Follow these steps to update your Branch avatar:

1. On the **Profile** tab, select the **Choose File** button.
2. Choose the file you want to upload.
3. Select **Save changes**.

### Personalization

You can set your role, industry, and primary goal on the **Profile** tab. Setting these helps Branch personalize your experience.
