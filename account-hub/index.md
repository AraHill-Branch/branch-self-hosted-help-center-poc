Welcome to Branch's **Account Hub**, your resource for managing your Branch account, configuring settings, and administering user access across your organization.

## What is Account Hub?

Account Hub is a dedicated documentation workspace designed for account administrators, team managers, and users responsible for configuring and maintaining Branch settings. Whether you're setting up a new account, managing user permissions, or configuring link settings, you'll find step-by-step guides and best practices here to help manage your Branch implementation.

This hub focuses on account administration and configuration, from initial setup and app settings to user management, security, and billing.

## How to navigate

Use the left sidebar to browse topics, or use the search function (CMD+K) to quickly find specific information. Each section contains configuration guides, administrative instructions, and best practices to help you manage your Branch account effectively.

Article folders are organized by administrative function, making it easy to find the settings and tools you need to configure and maintain your account.
