## Overview

The way you view your invoices will depend on whether your Dashboard uses Branch’s new or legacy billing experience, as well as whether you are an enterprise or self-serve customer.

An invoice will be sent via email to the billing contact of your account with details of the charge and a copy of the invoice receipt.

::: danger Warning
Please note that this article explains the [legacy Branch experience](branch-dashboard-overview.md) for managing invoices, which is slated for deprecation.  
  
In the [new version of Branch](new-branch-overview.md), attribution windows are configured under **Account** → [**Billing**](billing-invoices-new.md).
:::

## Self-serve invoices

For **self-serve** customers, you can glance at your invoices through the **Billing** [tab](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings/billing) of the Branch Dashboard in the **Invoice Details** section. From here, you can quickly view the invoice amount and download a copy of the invoice.



After each invoice is generated, it will automatically be paid using your credit card on file.

If your billing contact is no longer at your company or you need assistance accessing copies of your invoices, please reach out to [billing@branch.io](mailto:billing@branch.io).

## Enterprise invoices

For **Enterprise** customers, you can get a comprehensive view of your invoices through the **Billing** [tab](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings/billing) of the **Account Settings** page of the Branch Dashboard.

Here you will be able to organize your invoices by:

- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date, Service Period, and Due Date
- Invoice Balance
- Whether the invoice is for multiple apps

You can also download a copy of your invoice (PDF).



If you have any questions regarding your invoice, reach out to your account manager or to [billing@branch.io](mailto:billing@branch.io).
