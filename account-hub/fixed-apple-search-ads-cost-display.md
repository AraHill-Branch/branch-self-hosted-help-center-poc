## Overview

We have updated the logic for how **cost** is displayed when selecting any of the dimensions `ad id/name` or `creative id/name` but not including `keyword/keyword id`. Before, it would only show data for rows where the ad group has targeted a custom product page and 0 for default product pages.

Now it will show the cost for **both** types of product pages.
