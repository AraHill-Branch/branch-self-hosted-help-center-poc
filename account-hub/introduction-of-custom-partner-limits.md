---
title: "Introduction of Custom Partner Limits"
slug: introduction-of-custom-partner-limits
---

As of October 2023, the number of Custom Partners that can be enabled on a Branch account is capped at 25.



When your account reaches this limit, you will be asked to disable or delete a Custom Partner before you can enable a new one.

::: tip
For more information about adding a Custom Partner, visit Branch's [guide](adding-custom-partners.md).
:::
