The highly anticipated GA release of ROI Hub is finally here! ROI Hub centralizes all your cost and attributed revenue data, eliminating the need for manual consolidation.



## Overview

ROI Hub helps you get comprehensive return on investment insights and analyze cost data ingestion with Branch.

With improved cost data support and the ability to upload cost in several different ways, you have access to 100% cost coverage across all your ad partners.

Plus, granular reporting and automated alerts for connection issues keep you one step ahead, allowing you to focus on optimizing growth!

## Upload Cost Data

Branch offers various ways for you to upload cost data for analysis. To get this data to Branch, use one of the following methods:

- [File Upload](cost-data-via-file-upload.md)
- [Direct Integration](cost-data-via-api-connection.md)
- [Scheduled Email Ingestion](cost-data-via-scheduled-ingestion.md)
- [Ad Link Parameter Tracking](cost-on-links.md)
