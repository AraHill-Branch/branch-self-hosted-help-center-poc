## Overview

We’ve expanded access to the unified **Links** tab to all users. You will now see a single unified page to manage Short Links and Ad Links. You’ll also get access to faster searching and and more efficient filtering.

## Get started

In the Branch Dashboard, select **Links** from the left navigation panel.

From here, you can manage, filter, and create Short Links and Ad Links.
