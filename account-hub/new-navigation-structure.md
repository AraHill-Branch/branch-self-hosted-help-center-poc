## Overview

We’ve updated the [Branch Dashboard](https://branch.dashboard.branch.io/) navigation to make it more modern, intuitive, and easier to navigate. These changes are designed to help you find what you need faster by improving the overall experience.

## New sections

There are now four main sections:

- **Analysis**: View metrics for campaigns, fraud, ads, and more.
- **Campaigns**: Manage links, Journeys, QR codes, DeepViews, and more.
- **Configure**: Manage app configuration, ROI Hub, ad partners, and more.
- **Account**: Manage your account and team settings.

## Main navigation changes

- Added **Analysis** and **Campaign** sections to organize pages around core workflows
- Removed **Cross-Channel Analytics** section
- Moved **Summary**, **Sources**, and **Live View** to the **Overview** page in the **Analysis** section
- Moved **Ads Analytics** to the **Analysis** section
- Moved **ROI Hub** to the **Configuration** section
- Moved **Journeys**, **Emails**, **LinkHub**, **QR Codes**, and **Engagement Builder** to the **Campaigns** section
- Moved **Exports** to the **Configure** section
- Moved configuration items to the **App Settings** page in the **Configure** section
