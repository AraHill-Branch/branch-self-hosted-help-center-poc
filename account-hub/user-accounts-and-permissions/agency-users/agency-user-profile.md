---
title: "Manage agency user profile"
slug: agency-user-profile
---

On your agency user profile page, you can manage your name, credentials, and access your Dashboard UID. To make changes to your user profile:

1. In Branch, go to the [Account Settings](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings) page, and select the [User](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings/user) tab, where you can edit the following:

   - **First and Last Names**
   - **Change Password**
2. Select **Save**.

   *[Image: 1972]*

::: danger Warning
To change the email address associated with an existing agency team member, contact [Support](https://support.branch.io/hc/en-us/requests/new).
:::
