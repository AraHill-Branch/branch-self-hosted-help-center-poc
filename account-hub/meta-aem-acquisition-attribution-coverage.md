Branch is expanding our ongoing support for our integration with Meta’s Aggregated Event Measurement (AEM) protocol, which helps you maximize your performance measurement and the value of your campaigns.

## Overview

Over the past year, Branch has worked closely with Meta to support their enhancements to AEM. These enhancements help marketers with re-engagement optimization, acquisition optimization, and re-engagement attribution.

## What's New?

Branch is expanding our integration with Meta even further by adding support for AEM acquisition attribution – this will give you even deeper insights and empower you to make better data-driven decisions across your acquisition campaigns.

To achieve optimal attribution coverage with Meta AEM, we recommend enabling our new [Predictive Aggregate Measurement (PAM)](predictive-aggregate-measurement.md) feature, which increases iOS attribution coverage in a privacy-centric manner.

## Release Date

Support for acquisition attribution will start on **November 15, 2024**.

You will see an increase in your Meta campaign data within the Branch Dashboard, which will allow you to derive more precise insights.

**No action is required** from you to turn this on.

## Learn More

To learn more about using Branch with Meta's AEM protocol, visit our [guide](enable-app-aggregated-event-measurement-support.md).

If you have any questions or would like to discuss how this update benefits your team, we encourage you to reach out to your Meta Account team.
