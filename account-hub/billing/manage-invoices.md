## Overview

The way you view your invoices depends on whether you're using Branch's new or legacy billing experience, as well as whether you're an enterprise or self-serve customer.

Branch sends an invoice via email to the billing contact of your account with details of the charge and a copy of the invoice receipt.

::: danger Warning
Please note that this article explains the [legacy Branch experience](branch-dashboard-overview.md) for managing invoices, which is slated for deprecation.  
  
In the [new version of Branch](new-branch-overview.md), attribution windows are configured under **Account** → [**Billing**](billing-invoices-new.md).
:::

## Self-serve invoices

For **self-serve** customers, you can glance at your invoices through the **Billing** [tab](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings/billing) of Branch in the **Invoice Details** section. From here, you can quickly view the invoice amount and download a copy of the invoice.



After each invoice is generated, it's automatically paid using your credit card on file.

If your billing contact is no longer at your company or you need assistance accessing copies of your invoices, please reach out to [billing@branch.io](mailto:billing@branch.io).

## Enterprise invoices

For **Enterprise** customers, you can get a comprehensive view of your invoices through the **Billing** [tab](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings/billing) of the **Account Settings** page in Branch.

Here you can organize your invoices by:

- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date, Service Period, and Due Date
- Invoice Balance
- Whether the invoice is for multiple apps

You can also download a copy of your invoice (PDF).



If you have any questions regarding your invoice, reach out to your account manager or to [billing@branch.io](mailto:billing@branch.io).
