We've enhanced analytics and Engagement Builder with a new filter: App Version. Learn more here! 👏

## Overview

Engagement Builder is a powerful tool in Branch that allow you to build audiences to enhance your marketing campaigns. You could create audiences that target:

- Platform (Android or iOS)
- Country
- Rule

Now you can target by **App Version**.



App Versions is also available in ads analytics Event-Level and Cohorts (not available in Unified Analytics) via Trends chart and Events table.
