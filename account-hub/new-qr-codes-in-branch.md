Create, customize, and manage Branch-powered QR Codes. Learn more about this new feature here ✨

## Overview

You can now create individual QR Codes in the Branch Dashboard. Additionally, you'll be able to customize, organize/manage, download, and view campaign performance.

::: info Access
Branch QR Codes requires Enterprise platform access. Please reach out to your account manager or <https://branch.io/pricing> to activate.
:::
