Branch has added currency configuration capabilities to Analysis Dashboards, giving you the flexibility to view your revenue and cost metrics in your preferred currency for better regional analysis and reporting.

## What’s new?

Analysis Dashboards now include the following currency functionality:

- **Dashboard-level currency**: Set a default currency parameter that applies across all widgets on your dashboard containing currency-related measures.
- **Widget-level currency**: Override the dashboard currency for individual widgets, allowing you to compare performance across different currencies on the same dashboard.
- **Comprehensive currency support**: Choose from a list of over 30 currencies to match your business needs and regional reporting requirements.

## Benefits

Currency configuration enables you to:

- View campaign performance in the currencies most relevant to your regional teams and stakeholders.
- Maintain a single currency standard across your dashboard for clearer performance comparisons.
- Create duplicate dashboards with different currency settings when you need to analyze performance across multiple markets simultaneously.

## Access

Currency configuration support is available to all Analysis Dashboard users.

Analysis Dashboards represent the next evolution in Branch's marketing analytics and reporting. They unify data from ads, social media, SEO, email, and more into a single, cohesive experience.

Access to this feature is currently being rolled out to customers in phases as part of Branch's new platform experience.
