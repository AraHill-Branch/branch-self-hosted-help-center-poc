---
title: "Manage Agency Profile"
slug: agency-profile
---

Agency Admins can access and edit the Profile tab. You can edit your Agency name, and access your Agency ID. To do this:

1. From the Branch dashboard, navigate to the [Account Settings](https://dashboard.branch.io/account-settings) page, and click to the **Profile** tab.
2. Here you can edit your **Agency Name**, and access your **Agency ID** (this is assigned by Branch and cannot be changed).
3. If you make any changes, click **Save**.
