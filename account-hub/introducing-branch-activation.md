## Overview

With Branch Activation, you can unify all your enterprise linking needs in one secure, scalable solution with app Short Links, web Short Links, and QR codes.

## Benefits

- Improve clickthrough rates and engagement with short, branded, trackable links in social posts and profiles.
- Create and manage web and app links and QR codes in one platform to reduce redundancy and unify analytics across use cases.
- Improve efficiency and gain control with centralized link governance, domain management, and role-based permissions.
- Instill trust and boost clickthrough rates with recognizable branded links and QR codes.

## Use cases

- **Social**: Improve clickthrough rates and engagement with short, branded, trackable links in social posts and profiles.
- **QR:** Customize QR code logos, colors, and shapes to drive and track digital actions from offline with up to 80% better performance.
- **SMS**: Short, smart links save cost and improve clickthrough rates while reliably taking users to the right content on web or app.
- **Sharing**: Enable easy content distribution from your share sheets, and learn where and how your content is spreading.
