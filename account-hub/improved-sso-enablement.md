We have improved the process for enabling Single Sign On (SSO) for your Branch app. Find out more here 👍

## Overview

You can now enable SSO for your Branch app without needing to reach out to Branch Support to set your Identity Provider (IdP) settings in Branch. This allows you to reduce the back-and-forth and set up your account much faster!

#### Prerequisites

1. The IdP you are using supports SAML (ex. Okta, OneLogin, Microsoft Azure AD, etc.)
2. Your company's IT department ready to configure SAML that knows your company's email domains
3. Admin Access Level to your Branch account
